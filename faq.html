Q: What is the Dark Web?

A: More data has been generated in the last two years than in the rest of human history; most of which is held online in public websites, databases, blogs and more.

Unknown to many, the Internet accounts for less than 1% of the entire World Wide Web, and more than 99% is hidden beneath the surface in the Dark Web.

The Dark Web is a network of websites and material similar to the regular Internet, but which mask/hide the IP address of the servers that host them. The websites and material on the Dark Web is not indexed by search engines. Due to this, it is a haven for criminal activity.
